CleanClicks connects to Microsoft Ads (Bing) via OAuth for offline conversion uploads through the Microsoft Ads API.

## Prerequisites

- A Microsoft Advertising account
- Admin or Standard access to the account
- At least one conversion goal set up in Microsoft Ads

## Connecting

1. Go to **Connections** in your CleanClicks dashboard
2. Select your domain
3. Find **Microsoft Ads** in the One-Click Connect section
4. Click **Connect**
5. Sign in with your Microsoft account (the one linked to your Microsoft Ads account)
6. Authorize CleanClicks
7. You'll be redirected back with a success message

## Configuring the Vendor

After connecting:

1. Go to **Configuration** > **Vendors** tab
2. Enable **Microsoft Ads**
3. Enter your **Customer ID** and **Account ID** (both visible in the top-right of the Microsoft Ads dashboard)
4. Save

### Finding Your IDs

In the Microsoft Ads interface, look at the top-right corner:
- **Customer ID** — the top-level account identifier
- **Account ID** — the specific ad account (you may have multiple under one customer)

## How It Works

When a visitor converts:
1. CleanClicks captures the `msclkid` (Microsoft Click ID) from the landing page URL
2. The conversion event is sent server-side to Microsoft's Offline Conversions API
3. Microsoft matches the conversion using the msclkid and/or hashed email
4. The conversion is attributed to the correct ad click, keyword, and campaign

### Auto-Tagging

For msclkid capture to work, make sure **Auto-tagging** is enabled in Microsoft Ads:
1. Go to Microsoft Ads > **Settings** > **Account Settings**
2. Under **Auto-tagging**, ensure it's turned on

Without auto-tagging, there's no msclkid to capture and conversions can only be matched via email.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|---|---|
| OAuth fails | Verify you're signing in with the Microsoft account that has access to your ad account |
| "Needs Reconnection" | Disconnect and reconnect to refresh credentials |
| Conversions not appearing | Verify Customer ID and Account ID. Check that auto-tagging is enabled. Allow 24-48 hours for processing. |
| Wrong account connected | Disconnect, then reconnect and choose the correct Microsoft account during OAuth |
