CleanClicks connects to LinkedIn via OAuth for automatic conversion uploads through the LinkedIn Conversions API.

## Prerequisites

- A LinkedIn Campaign Manager account
- At least one conversion action created in Campaign Manager
- Admin access to the LinkedIn ad account

## Connecting

1. Go to **Connections** in your CleanClicks dashboard
2. Select your domain
3. Find **LinkedIn** in the One-Click Connect section
4. Click **Connect**
5. Sign in with your LinkedIn account
6. Authorize CleanClicks
7. You'll be redirected back with a success message

## Configuring the Vendor

After connecting:

1. Go to **Configuration** > **Vendors** tab
2. Enable **LinkedIn**
3. Enter your **Ad Account ID** (visible in Campaign Manager URL or account settings)
4. Enter your **Conversion Action URN** (from Campaign Manager > Analyze > Conversions)
5. Save

### Finding Your Conversion Action URN

1. In LinkedIn Campaign Manager, go to **Analyze** > **Conversion tracking**
2. Click on the conversion action you want CleanClicks to report to
3. The URN is in the URL or details panel (format: `urn:li:lyndaConversionAction:XXXXXXXXXX` or similar)

## How It Works

When a visitor converts:
1. CleanClicks captures the `li_fat_id` (LinkedIn First-Party Ad Tracking ID) from cookies
2. The conversion event is sent server-side to LinkedIn's Conversions API
3. LinkedIn matches the conversion to the original ad interaction
4. Customer email is sent as a SHA-256 hash for matching

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|---|---|
| OAuth fails | Ensure you have admin access to the LinkedIn ad account |
| "Needs Reconnection" | Token may have expired. Disconnect and reconnect. |
| Conversions not appearing | Verify Ad Account ID and Conversion Action URN. LinkedIn can take 24-48 hours to reflect offline conversions. |
| Permission denied | Your LinkedIn account needs Marketing API access on the associated company page |
