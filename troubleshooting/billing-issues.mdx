Payment and subscription problems and how to resolve them.

## Payment Failed

If a payment fails, your subscription enters a **past due** state. Tracking continues while Stripe retries the payment.

**To fix:**
1. Go to **Billing** in CleanClicks
2. Click **Manage Billing** to open the Stripe portal
3. Update your payment method
4. Stripe will retry the charge automatically

If all payment retries fail, your subscription may be suspended. Update your payment method to reactivate.

## Can't Complete Checkout

If the Stripe checkout page won't load or payment won't process:

- **Browser issue:** Try a different browser or Incognito mode
- **Ad blocker:** Some ad blockers interfere with Stripe's checkout. Disable it temporarily.
- **Card declined:** Try a different payment method. Contact your bank if your card is being incorrectly declined.
- **Still stuck:** Contact [helpdesk@cleanclicks.io](mailto:helpdesk@cleanclicks.io)

## I Was Charged More Than Expected

Extra charges may come from:

- **Plan change:** Upgrading mid-cycle incurs a prorated charge.

(Session overages are shown on your dashboard but are not auto-charged today -- an unexpected charge is almost always a mid-cycle plan change.)

View your full invoice in **Billing** > **Manage Billing** to see the line-item breakdown.

## I Want to Cancel

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Billing**
3. Click **Cancel subscription** in the Stripe portal
4. Your account stays active until the end of the billing period

After cancellation:
- Tracking stops at period end
- Configuration is retained for 90 days
- You can resubscribe anytime to pick up where you left off

## I Canceled but Was Still Charged

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the billing period, not immediately. If you canceled on the 5th and your period runs through the 28th, you'll have access (and won't be refunded for) the remaining time.

If you believe a charge is incorrect, contact [helpdesk@cleanclicks.io](mailto:helpdesk@cleanclicks.io) with the invoice details.

## Switching Between Monthly and Annual

1. Go to **Billing** > **Manage Billing** (Stripe portal)
2. Update your subscription's billing interval
3. Annual billing saves 10%

## I Need an Invoice for Accounting

All invoices are available in the Stripe billing portal:

1. Go to **Billing** > **Manage Billing**
2. Scroll to **Invoice history**
3. Click any invoice to view or download a PDF

Invoices include your company name, plan details, and all charges.

## Session Overages

If you're consistently exceeding your session limit:

| Your Plan | Monthly Limit | Overage Rate | Consider Upgrading To |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pulse | 10,000 | $8/1K | Signal (50K) |
| Signal | 50,000 | $6/1K | Clarity (250K) |
| Clarity | 250,000 | $4/1K | Command (unlimited) |

To check your current usage, look at the dashboard home page. If overages are a regular occurrence, upgrading is usually more cost-effective.
