Your domain is the foundation of CleanClicks. Every tracking tag, conversion event, and platform connection is scoped to a specific domain.

## Adding Your First Domain

If you're going through the onboarding wizard, Step 2 handles this automatically. Otherwise:

1. Go to **Domains** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Domain**
3. Enter your root domain (e.g., `yourdomain.com`)
4. Click **Add**

### Domain Format

- Enter the **bare domain** without `https://` or `www`. Just `yourdomain.com`.
- If your site runs on a subdomain (like `shop.yourdomain.com`), enter the full subdomain.
- Domain names must contain only lowercase letters, numbers, hyphens, and dots.

## Domain Statuses

After adding a domain, it goes through these states:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending Verification** | Domain added but CNAME not yet detected. Tracking won't work yet. |
| **Active** | CNAME verified. Tracking is live. |
| **Paused** | You've paused tracking for this domain. Configuration is read-only. |

## Plan Limits

Each plan limits the number of domains you can track:

| Plan | Max Domains |
|---|---|
| Pulse | 1 |
| Signal | 1 |
| Clarity | 3 |
| Command | Unlimited |

If you've hit your limit, the **Add Domain** button will be disabled with a note to upgrade.

## Pausing a Domain

If you need to temporarily stop tracking on a domain without deleting it:

1. Go to **Domains**
2. Find the domain card
3. Click **Pause**

Paused domains retain all their configuration but stop processing traffic. Configuration pages become read-only. Resume anytime to restart tracking.

## Deleting a Domain

Deleting a domain removes it and all associated configuration permanently. This action cannot be undone.

1. Go to **Domains**
2. Find the domain card
3. Click **Delete** and confirm

> **Warning:** Deleting a domain also removes all connected platform tokens and API keys for that domain.
