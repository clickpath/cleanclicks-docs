The Analytics Add-On ($299/mo) extends Pulse and Signal plans with analytics platform support and the full traffic quality dashboard. It's already included in Clarity and Command plans.

## What It Unlocks

| Feature | Without Add-On | With Add-On |
|---|---|---|
| GA4 (Measurement Protocol + Proxy) | Not available | Enabled |
| Usermaven (server-side + proxy) | Not available | Enabled |
| Plerdy (analytics proxy) | Not available | Enabled |
| Traffic Analytics depth | Pulse: none / Signal: basic | Full (all periods, all sections) |

## Important: Platform Counting

GA4 and Usermaven **count against your plan's ad platform limit**, not a separate analytics quota. For example:

- On Signal (2 platform limit) with the Add-On: you could use Google Ads + GA4, or Meta + Usermaven, but not all four.
- On Pulse (1 platform limit) with the Add-On: you could use GA4 *or* one ad platform, not both.

Plan your platform selections accordingly, or upgrade to Clarity for up to 7 platforms.

## Adding the Analytics Add-On

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Find the Analytics Add-On section
3. Click **Add** and confirm
4. The add-on takes effect immediately

## Removing the Analytics Add-On

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Click **Remove** on the Analytics Add-On
3. Removal takes effect at the end of your current billing period

When removed, GA4 and Usermaven configurations are retained but become inactive. If you re-add the add-on, they'll work again without reconfiguration.

## Pricing

- **Monthly:** $299/mo
- **Annual:** Matches your plan's billing cycle

## Who Should Get It?

The Add-On is most valuable if you:

- Use GA4 and want ad-blocker bypass for analytics data
- Want the full traffic analytics dashboard on Signal
- Need Usermaven or Plerdy proxying on a lower-tier plan

If you're running multiple ad platforms *and* analytics platforms, Clarity ($1,299/mo with everything included) is often more cost-effective than Signal + Add-On ($898/mo combined) given the expanded platform limit and ecommerce features.
