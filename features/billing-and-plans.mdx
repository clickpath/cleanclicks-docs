Manage your subscription, view invoices, and change plans from the Billing page.

## Accessing Billing

Go to **Billing** in the sidebar.

## Your Subscription

The Billing page shows:

- **Current plan** — which tier you're on (Pulse, Signal, Clarity, or Command)
- **Billing cycle** — monthly or annual
- **Current period** — when your billing period started and when it renews
- **Status** — active, trialing, past due, or canceled

## Choosing a Plan

During or after your trial, select a plan:

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Review the plan cards with features and pricing
3. Click **Subscribe** on your chosen plan
4. Choose monthly or annual billing (annual saves 10%)
5. Complete checkout via Stripe

Stripe handles all payment processing. CleanClicks never sees or stores your credit card details.

## Changing Plans

### Upgrading
Click **Upgrade** on a higher-tier plan. The upgrade takes effect immediately. You're charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing period.

### Downgrading
Click **Downgrade** on a lower-tier plan. The downgrade takes effect at the **end of your current billing period**. You keep full access to your current tier until then.

### Switching Billing Cycle
To switch between monthly and annual (or vice versa), use the Stripe billing portal (accessible from the Billing page).

## Invoices

View and download past invoices from the Billing page. Invoices are generated by Stripe and include:

- Plan charges
- Overage charges (if applicable)

## Managing Payment

Click **Manage Billing** to open the Stripe billing portal where you can:

- Update payment method
- Change billing address
- View full invoice history
- Cancel subscription

## Trial Period

New accounts start with a **14-day trial**. During the trial:

- Full access to all platform features
- No credit card required
- All plans available to test

When the trial ends, you'll need to subscribe to continue using CleanClicks.

## Overages

If you exceed your plan's monthly session limit, **tracking continues uninterrupted** -- we never stop collecting your data at the cap. Your plan's overage rate is:

| Plan | Overage Rate |
|---|---|
| Pulse | $8 per 1,000 sessions |
| Signal | $6 per 1,000 sessions |
| Clarity | $4 per 1,000 sessions |
| Command | No overages |

Overage usage is shown on your dashboard. If you're regularly over your limit, upgrading is usually the better deal -- reach out and we'll help you size the right plan.

## Cancellation

To cancel your subscription:

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Billing** to open the Stripe portal
3. Click **Cancel subscription**

Your account remains active until the end of the current billing period. After that, tracking stops and your configuration is retained for 90 days in case you resubscribe.
