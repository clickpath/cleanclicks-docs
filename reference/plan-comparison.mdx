## Supported Ad Platforms

All plans support all platforms (subject to connection limit):

- Google Ads (OAuth)
- Meta / Facebook (manual token)
- TikTok (manual token)
- Microsoft Ads / Bing (OAuth)
- GA4 (API key, requires Clarity+ or Analytics Add-On)
- Usermaven (API key, requires Clarity+ or Analytics Add-On)

## Analytics Add-On ($299/mo)

Available for Pulse and Signal plans. Included in Clarity and Command.

| What It Unlocks | Details |
|---|---|
| GA4 as a configurable platform | Measurement Protocol + first-party proxy |
| Usermaven as a configurable platform | Server-side events + first-party proxy |
| Plerdy analytics proxy | First-party script loading |
| Full traffic analytics | All time periods, all dashboard sections |

**Note:** GA4 and Usermaven count against your ad platform limit. On Pulse (1 platform), the Add-On lets you use GA4 *or* one ad platform, not both.

## Choosing the Right Plan

### You need Pulse if:
- One website, one ad platform, moderate traffic (under 10K sessions/mo)
- You want clean conversion data without complexity

### You need Signal if:
- Running ads on 2 platforms
- You want traffic quality visibility and geographic filtering
- 50K sessions/mo or less

### You need Clarity if:
- Running ecommerce (Shopify or WooCommerce)
- Multiple ad platforms (up to 7)
- You need analytics proxying for ad-blocker bypass
- Multiple domains (up to 3)

### You need Command if:
- Agency managing many client domains
- High volume (250K+ sessions/mo)
- Need unlimited scale with no overage risk
