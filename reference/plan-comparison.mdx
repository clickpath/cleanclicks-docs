## Supported Ad Platforms

All plans support all platforms (subject to connection limit):

- Google Ads (OAuth)
- Meta / Facebook (manual token)
- TikTok (manual token)
- Microsoft Ads / Bing (OAuth)
- GA4 (API key, requires Signal+)
- Usermaven (API key, requires Signal+)

GA4 and Usermaven are analytics-layer connections (Signal and up) and do not count against your ad-platform connection limit.

## Choosing the Right Plan

### You need Pulse if:
- One website, one ad platform, moderate traffic (under 10K sessions/mo)
- You want clean conversion data without complexity

### You need Signal if:
- Running ads on up to 5 platforms
- Selling online (Shopify or WooCommerce) and want ecommerce conversion tracking
- You want traffic quality visibility and geographic filtering
- 50K sessions/mo or less

### You need Clarity if:
- Multiple domains (up to 3) or up to 250K sessions/mo
- You need health monitoring and the GA4 + First-Party Delta analytics dashboard
- Everything in Signal, at greater scale

### You need Command if:
- Agency managing many client domains
- High volume (250K+ sessions/mo)
- Need unlimited scale with no overage risk
