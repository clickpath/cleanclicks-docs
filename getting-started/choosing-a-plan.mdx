## Which Plan Is Right for You?

### Pulse ($199/mo)
Best for: **Single-domain businesses** running one primary ad platform (usually Google Ads) with moderate traffic. You want clean conversion data without the complexity.

Good fit if: You have one website, one main ad platform, and under 10,000 tracked sessions per month.

### Signal ($399/mo)
Best for: **Growing businesses** running ads on several platforms (up to 5) that need traffic quality insights, geographic filtering, and ecommerce conversion tracking. The traffic analytics dashboard helps you understand what's real and what's noise.

Good fit if: You're running Google Ads plus Meta (or other platforms), selling online (Shopify or WooCommerce), want to filter out-of-market traffic, and need traffic quality reporting.

### Clarity ($899/mo)
Best for: **Multi-domain businesses** that need more scale and the deepest visibility. Everything in Signal, plus up to 3 domains, 250,000 sessions per month, health monitoring, and the GA4 + First-Party Delta analytics dashboard (the dollar value of conversions GA4 missed).

Good fit if: You're running serious ad spend, manage more than one site, and need health monitoring plus delta analytics on top of the Signal feature set.

### Command (Custom)
Best for: **Agencies and enterprise** managing many domains with high volume. Unlimited domains, unlimited sessions, and dedicated support.

Contact [helpdesk@cleanclicks.io](mailto:helpdesk@cleanclicks.io) for Command pricing.

## Overages

If you exceed your monthly session limit, **tracking continues uninterrupted** -- we never stop collecting your data at the cap. Your plan's overage rate:

- Pulse: $8 per 1,000 additional sessions
- Signal: $6 per 1,000 additional sessions
- Clarity: $4 per 1,000 additional sessions
- Command: No overages

Overage usage is shown on your dashboard home page. If you're regularly over your limit, upgrading is usually the better deal.

## Trial Period

Every plan starts with a **14-day free trial**. Full access, no credit card required upfront. When the trial ends, you'll choose a plan and enter payment information to continue.

## Changing Plans

You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from the **Billing** page in your dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
