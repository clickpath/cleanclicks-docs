## Which Plan Is Right for You?

### Pulse ($199/mo)
Best for: **Single-domain businesses** running one primary ad platform (usually Google Ads) with moderate traffic. You want clean conversion data without the complexity.

Good fit if: You have one website, one main ad platform, and under 10,000 tracked sessions per month.

### Signal ($599/mo)
Best for: **Growing businesses** running ads on two platforms that need traffic quality insights and geographic filtering. The traffic analytics dashboard helps you understand what's real and what's noise.

Good fit if: You're running Google Ads plus Meta (or another platform), want to filter out-of-market traffic, and need basic traffic quality reporting.

### Clarity ($1,299/mo)
Best for: **Established ecommerce brands and multi-domain businesses** that need the full stack. Ecommerce tracking (Shopify/WooCommerce), analytics proxying (GA4, Usermaven, Plerdy through your domain), health monitoring, and up to 3 domains.

Good fit if: You're running serious ad spend across multiple platforms, selling online, and need complete visibility into traffic quality and conversion attribution.

### Command (Custom)
Best for: **Agencies and enterprise** managing many domains with high volume. Unlimited domains, unlimited sessions, and dedicated support.

Contact [helpdesk@cleanclicks.io](mailto:helpdesk@cleanclicks.io) for Command pricing.

## Overages

If you exceed your monthly session limit, tracking continues uninterrupted. You'll be billed for overages at your plan's rate:

- Pulse: $8 per 1,000 additional sessions
- Signal: $6 per 1,000 additional sessions
- Clarity: $4 per 1,000 additional sessions
- Command: No overages

You can monitor your session usage on the dashboard home page.

## Trial Period

Every plan starts with a **14-day free trial**. Full access, no credit card required upfront. When the trial ends, you'll choose a plan and enter payment information to continue.

## Changing Plans

You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from the **Billing** page in your dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
