Getting started with CleanClicks takes about two minutes. No credit card required for the 14-day trial.

## Sign Up

Visit [platform.cleanclicks.io](https://platform.cleanclicks.io) and choose one of two sign-up methods:

### Magic Link (Email)

1. Enter your email address
2. Check your inbox for a sign-in link (arrives within a few seconds)
3. Click the link to access your account

The magic link expires after 15 minutes. If it expires, just request a new one. No password to remember or manage.

### Google Sign-In

1. Click **Sign in with Google**
2. Choose your Google account
3. You're in

Google sign-in uses your Google account's verified email. If you later want to sign in via magic link instead, use the same email address and both methods will access the same account.

## What Happens After Sign-Up

When you first sign in, CleanClicks creates your account with a **14-day free trial**. During the trial you have full access to explore the platform, set up your domain, and test your tracking configuration.

The onboarding wizard launches automatically to walk you through the essential setup steps. You can complete it in one sitting or come back to it later.

## Account Details

Your account is tied to your email address. A few things to know:

- **One account per email.** If you need separate accounts for different businesses, use different email addresses.
- **Your name and company** can be updated anytime in Settings.
- **Sessions are secure.** You'll stay logged in for 7 days. After that, you'll need to sign in again.

## Signing In Later

Return to [platform.cleanclicks.io](https://platform.cleanclicks.io) anytime and sign in with either method (magic link or Google). Both point to the same account as long as the email matches.

If you're having trouble signing in, see [Common Issues](/troubleshooting/common-issues).
