CleanClicks is a first-party conversion tracking platform. It routes your ad platform data, analytics, and conversion events through your own domain instead of third-party cookies and scripts that browsers increasingly block.

## The Problem

If you're running paid ads, you've probably noticed the data getting worse. Conversions don't match between platforms. Attribution gaps keep growing. The culprit: browser privacy features, ad blockers, and the slow death of third-party cookies are all eroding the data your ad platforms depend on.

When a visitor clicks your Google Ad and lands on your site, the tracking pixel fires from a third-party domain. Safari, Firefox, and a growing number of Chrome users either block that request entirely or limit the cookie to 24 hours. Your conversion? Gone. Your optimization data? Incomplete. Your ROAS reporting? Fiction.

## How CleanClicks Fixes It

CleanClicks creates a first-party tracking subdomain on your website (like `cleanclicks.yourdomain.com`). All tracking data flows through this subdomain instead of third-party domains. To the browser, it looks like your own website talking to itself, because it is.

This means:
- **Ad blockers don't block it.** First-party requests sail through.
- **Cookies last as long as they should.** No 24-hour ITP limits.
- **Conversion data reaches your ad platforms.** Server-side relay ensures delivery even when client-side scripts are blocked.
- **Your analytics stay accurate.** GA4, Usermaven, and Plerdy data proxied through your domain.

## What It Supports

CleanClicks works with the major ad and analytics platforms:

- **Ad Platforms:** Google Ads, Meta (Facebook), TikTok, LinkedIn, Microsoft Ads (Bing)
- **Analytics:** GA4, Usermaven, Plerdy
- **Ecommerce:** Shopify, WooCommerce

Each platform gets conversion data delivered server-side, so even if the visitor's browser blocks the original tracking script, the conversion still gets recorded.

## Who It's For

- **Performance marketers** who need accurate conversion data for campaign optimization
- **Ecommerce brands** losing revenue attribution to ad blockers and privacy restrictions
- **Agencies** managing multiple client domains that need reliable cross-platform tracking
- **Any business running paid ads** where the gap between actual conversions and reported conversions keeps growing

## How It Works (The Short Version)

1. You add your domain to CleanClicks
2. You create a DNS record that routes a tracking subdomain through CleanClicks
3. You install a lightweight script tag on your site
4. You connect your ad platforms
5. CleanClicks captures conversions first-party and relays them server-side to each platform

The whole setup takes about 15 minutes. The onboarding wizard walks you through every step.
